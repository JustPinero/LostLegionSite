About the project

After a cataclysmic solar storm ravages a once advanced civilization, the survivors on each of the inner planets plummet into simultaneous dark ages. With their former protectors, a race of advanced artificial intelligences now gone, barbarity and wanton savagery become commonplace.

Generations later, humanity is now split not only by planetary boundaries but by racial lines as well as the former colonists have followed their own evolutionary advances.

As technology is rediscovered it becomes a race to unlock the secrets of the past lest the future be stolen by rivals.
