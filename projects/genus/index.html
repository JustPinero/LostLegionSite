About the project

In the dense evergreen terrain of the Pacific Northwest, a forest scourge has taken hold where a brilliant, but erratic plant neurobiologist must lead a team to decipher its origins and ultimately destroy it. Tragically, she is stranded and must fight her way through hostile plant life, in efforts to escape the mutated forest that is spreading across the US. This a horror story about death and decay. It is about the perseverance of nature in spite of our human insolence. It is about personal retribution and with it, an unexpected rebirth.
