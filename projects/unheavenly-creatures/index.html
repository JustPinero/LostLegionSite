About the project

A gang of bank robbers threatens the stability of the hyper-authoritative Houses of the Star Supremacy. Growing in reputation, they choose to assault one of the Houses most heavily secured facility when something goes wrong and Sister Spider, the lover of the gang's leader Nostrand, is murdered.

Nostrand and the last member of the gang, Otto, are captured and transported to the planetary prison Dark Sentencer. But not is all that it seems as Nostrand discovers a secret that could be the thread that unravels the Star Supremacy's power over civilization.
